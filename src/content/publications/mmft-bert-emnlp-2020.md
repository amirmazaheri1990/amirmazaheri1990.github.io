Introduces MMFT-BERT (Multi-Modal Fusion Transformer with BERT) for Visual Question Answering, utilizing separate and combined multimodal (video and text) processing with BERT encodings and a novel fusion method. Achieved SOTA results on the TVQA dataset and created TVQA-Visual, a diagnostic subset to analyze the model's handling of both modalities.
