Introduces a few-shot framework for assessing wound healing to distinguish "healer" from "non-healer" wounds through temporal image analysis. Experimental results validate the framework's effectiveness in improving early intervention in wound care, demonstrating up to 92% accuracy. Through this work I co-advised two MS students from UC Santa Cruz.
